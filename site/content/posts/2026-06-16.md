## Top 5 This Week

1. **Computer-using agents in Copilot Studio hit GA** — UI-driven automation without brittle RPA scripts or API dependencies is now production-ready. If you have legacy LOB apps or vendor portals that have resisted automation, this changes the calculus. Evaluate governance and permissions boundaries before your business units do it without you.

2. **Entra Tenant Governance discovers shadow tenants** — Acquired companies, dev projects, and partner collaborations routinely leave orphaned tenants outside central IT visibility. This GA capability uses B2B collaboration signals, multitenant app registrations, and billing relationships to surface them. Run this now if you've never done a formal tenant inventory.

3. **Purview Data Security Posture Reports GA + Custom Workspaces in Preview** — You can now produce outcome-based reporting that answers "are our controls working?" rather than just streaming alert counts. The GA tier covers sensitivity label and DLP coverage; the preview tier adds custom workspaces and charts for executive-ready dashboards. Both warrant immediate evaluation against your compliance reporting requirements.

4. **Exchange 2016/2019 ESU Period 2 announced (May–October 2026)** — If you're still running on-prem Exchange 2016 or 2019, Period 1 ESU coverage ended April 2026. Period 2 buys you through October 2026 — but this is the last runway. Migration to Exchange SE or Exchange Online needs to be underway now.

5. **Entra ID Governance Account Discovery GA** — Apps connected to Entra already contain pre-existing users and permissions created outside governance workflows. Account Discovery surfaces those accounts from day one of connection, closing the visibility gap that lets access accumulate unchecked. Critical for any IGA rollout or app onboarding process.

## Identity

- **[Find Shadow Tenants and Reduce Risk Fast with Microsoft Entra Tenant Governance](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/microsoft-entra-blog/find-shadow-tenants-and-reduce-risk-fast-with-microsoft-entra/ba-p/4521996)** [GA] — Orphaned tenants from acquisitions, dev projects, and regional ops represent real, unmonitored attack surface. Entra Tenant Governance now discovers related tenants via B2B collaboration signals, multitenant app registrations, and shared billing relationships. Run a tenant discovery sweep and establish a governance baseline for any tenants you didn't know existed.

- **[You Can't Govern What You Can't See: Closing the Identity Visibility Gap for Apps](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/microsoft-entra-blog/you-can-t-govern-what-you-can-t-see-closing-the-identity/ba-p/4507464)** [GA] — Apps connected to Entra arrive with pre-existing accounts and permissions that were never provisioned through governance workflows — Account Discovery brings those into view immediately on connection. This directly addresses the access accumulation problem in SaaS and on-premises app onboarding. Factor this into your IGA app onboarding runbook.

- **[Reducing NTLM Dependency: IAKerb and LocalKDC in Windows Insider Preview](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/windows-it-pro-blog/reducing-ntlm-dependency-iakerb-and-localkdc-in-windows-insider/ba-p/4524615)** [Preview] — IAKerb and LocalKDC extend Kerberos authentication to scenarios that previously forced NTLM fallback, including local account and workgroup authentication. This is in Windows Insider Canary Channel now, targeting broader preview later this month. Start assessing your NTLM dependency with the NTLM audit events before this hits production rings.

- **[Secure External Attachments with Purview Encryption](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/microsoft-purview-blog/secure-external-attachments-with-purview-encryption/ba-p/4463114)** [GA] — When Purview encryption is applied to email attachments, the recipient experience is entirely dependent on your Conditional Access policies and guest account configuration. Enabling guest accounts gives a seamless experience but can generate thousands of guest objects; disabling them restricts external access. Review your CA policies and guest provisioning posture before deploying encrypted attachment workflows to external partners.

- **[Microsoft 365 Apps: Cloud Update — Enhanced Rollout Waves](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/roadmap?id=558251)** [Preview] — Cloud Update for Microsoft 365 Apps is getting support for more than three rollout waves and configurable inter-wave delays, using Entra ID groups.
